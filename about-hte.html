NORTHGATE TECHNOLOGIES INC

Ever since its establishment in 1990, Northgate has strived to deliver products and services that provide value to our customers, and become a company trusted and chosen by all of its partners.

Northgate Technologies, Inc. (Northgate) provides best-of-breed IT products, strategic IT solutions, and reliable IT services to SMEs and large enterprises across various industries in the Philippines.

MISSION

To place the organization in a position of strength by providing strategic technology solutions and excellent services that aim to make a significant impact on the IT industry to our clients — allowing them to become more efficient in their respective industries.

VISION

To become the most recognized IT solutions provider in the Philippines — making companies and organizations looking for ways to innovate and expand their business through technology choose us as their trusted IT partner.
